   20000    +0.172             0.172     0.486

=== FINDINGS ===
1. Delta_crit -> 0 with N (+0.17 at 20k to +0.01 at 400k), 8 seeds. The "bias floor" is dead;
   curv's residual is honest finite-N noise, not an irreducible offset. (Reconfirms the
   multi-seed refutation of Finding 3.)
2. RESOLUTION is COARSE and INTRINSIC. Delta_min ~0.24 at 100k, ~0.12 even at 400k. It is
   2*sqrt(2)*SD_seed(Delta), and SD_seed is dominated by the CSN marginal-exponent variance
   ((alpha-1)/(tau-1) amplifies tau,alpha estimation noise) -- NOT the 1/snz estimator. So a
   better 1/snz estimator cannot fix it; the floor on resolvable violations is set by how
   precisely tau and alpha can be measured at finite N.
3. A.2 catches GROSS violations (a 5-10% shuffle, V~0.2-0.5) at >=50k, but cannot resolve fine
   near-critical distinctions (Delta < ~0.12-0.24) at counts most markets will have (~1e3-1e5;
   only the very largest reach 1e6 where Delta_min approaches ~0.1).

=== RECOMMENDATION (for cross-review) ===
Demote Gate A.2 from a precise quantitative gate to a COARSE CORROBORATIVE check, WITH this
Delta_min(N) curve as the evidence (a measured finding, not a retreat). Concretely:
- The headline criticality evidence stays the branching ratio n (the project's stated primary
  object) + Gate A.1 (CSN power-law goodness-of-fit/LRT).
- A.2 reports whether the scaling relation is GROSSLY violated, with the achieved-N resolution
  Delta_min stated, as orthogonal support -- not a fine-threshold pass/fail.
- The reference-differenced-null machinery becomes UNNECESSARY at this resolution: a coarse
  corroborative check does not need bias-cancellation to ~0.04; report Delta and its CI
  descriptively. (The reference-null's honest residual value -- truth-free real data -- is
  noted but no longer load-bearing.)
- Pre-reg consequence: reword Gate A.2 / H3 / §5.3 / §7 from "Delta consistent with 0" (which a
  true critical generator does not hit precisely at finite N) to "scaling relation not grossly
  violated, resolution Delta_min(N) reported." This is a §6-plus-dependents change for Q2.

Still useful but no longer blocking: a cross-class cell (critical long-memory Hawkes, the
market-relevant class) to confirm the scaling relation holds for non-GW critical systems at all.
