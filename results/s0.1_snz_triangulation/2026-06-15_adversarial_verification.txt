=== VERIFIED CONCLUSIONS (these OVERTURN parts of the brief) ===
1. The "Delta ~0.09 bias floor" is NOT robust. With 6 seeds, curv's Delta = +0.029 (100k),
   +0.018 (200k) -- consistent with 0 and not growing. The earlier 0.09-0.13 came from
   single high-ratio seeds (13, 21). FINDING 3's premise (an irreducible floor forcing
   abandonment of the Delta=0 null) does not survive proper statistics. The reference-null
   was a fix for a problem that mostly is not there at achievable N.
2. Correction form is a large DOF (spread 0.47 across single-term forms). AIC prefers 1/sqrtT,
   but 1/sqrtT gives gamma=2.07 which makes the scaling relation FAIL (Delta=-0.16) because the
   CSN marginal ratio is itself finite-size-biased to ~1.90. The relation closes via MUTUAL
   bias consistency (1/T-curv ~ CSN-ratio ~ 1.88), so "pick the form that closes the relation"
   is the Delta-tuning trap. The principled estimator-selection rule is UNRESOLVED.
3. Shape-collapse (FINDING 2) was dropped on a biased objective: the shipped relative-CV
   objective is stuck at 1.65 even on clean profiles; a standard objective gives 1.82, peak
   1.90. The drop was unfair. (All estimators remain biased low at 100k on GW.)

=== ADVERSARIAL OBJECTIONS THAT STAND (logical, not requiring re-derivation) ===
A. Discriminating power UNTESTED: only the trivial full-shuffle look-alike (Delta~2) was run;
   whether the gate rejects a SMALL genuine violation (the near-critical regime that matters)
   was deferred to S0.2 while the brief wrote "verified". The headline is oversold. (4/5 reviewers)
B. Q2 (freeze §6) must HOLD, and the contamination is wider than §6: the Delta=0-achievable
   assumption is also in H3, §5.3, §7's Stage-2 rule, and §4f.
C. The reference-null adds researcher DOFs (which reference, matched-N, class, band width) the
   parameter-free Delta=0 null lacked; and the bias cancels only WITHIN universality class --
   real markets are long-memory Hawkes, not mean-field GW, so the fix may not transfer and could
   falsely reject a genuinely critical market. Unverified on any non-GW system.
D. B=25/50 bootstraps flip "contains 0" verdicts ~20-35% on seed; single-seed tables; the
   reference-gate delta-CI used an invalid element-wise subtraction (~10% too narrow); the two
   scripts use different size caps. Statistical rigor inadequate.
